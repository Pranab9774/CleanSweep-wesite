Frequently asked questions

Why does Deep AI Analysis take longer?

Deep AI Analysis performs more detailed checks and may take longer on devices with large storage or nearly full internal memory.

Can CleanSweep scan every Android folder?

Android limits direct access to some folders. For certain locations, users may need to select folders manually using Android’s folder access system.

Should I review files before deleting?

Yes. Always review scan results before deleting files, especially documents, photos, videos, and downloads.
