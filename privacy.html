Privacy-first

Your Privacy, Our Promise

CleanSweep is built to help you manage your storage while keeping your files on your device.

Important: CleanSweep does not upload your files, photos, videos, documents, or audio for scanning.

Information we process

CleanSweep analyzes files locally on your device to identify duplicate files, large files, outdated documents, storage categories, and cleanup suggestions.

On-device scanning

File scanning, duplicate detection, and cleanup recommendations are designed to happen on your phone. Your files are not sent to our servers for processing.

Analytics

We may use privacy-conscious analytics such as Firebase Analytics to understand app usage, improve reliability, and fix issues. Analytics events do not contain your personal files or file contents.

Deletion and recycle bin

When you delete files inside CleanSweep, they may be held in the in-app Recycle Bin for up to 30 days before permanent deletion, depending on the app version and device behavior.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at supportcleansweepapp@gmail.com.
