Privacy Policy

CleanSweep is an Android storage cleanup application designed to help users understand and manage device storage. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app may collect, how it is used, and the choices available to users.

1. Information we collect

CleanSweep may process file metadata on your device to identify duplicate files, outdated documents, large files, and storage recovery opportunities. This may include file names, file sizes, file types, folders selected by the user, scan status, and cleanup results.

CleanSweep may use Firebase Analytics or similar app analytics tools to collect app usage and performance information, such as screens viewed, scan events, feature usage, app errors, device type, operating system version, and general app engagement.

2. Information we do not intentionally collect

CleanSweep does not intentionally collect the contents of your personal files, photos, videos, documents, or media for advertising or resale. File scanning is designed to work on-device wherever possible.

3. How we use information

  • To provide storage scan results and cleanup suggestions.
  • To improve app reliability, performance, and user experience.
  • To understand which features are used and where users may face errors.
  • To provide support and respond to user requests.

4. Permissions

CleanSweep may request access to files, media, notifications, or selected folders depending on the feature being used. Permissions are used only to provide app functionality requested by the user.

5. Analytics and third-party services

CleanSweep may use Firebase Analytics and related Google services. These providers may process technical and usage data according to their own privacy terms. We use analytics to improve app stability and understand app usage, not to sell personal data.

6. Data sharing

We do not sell user personal information. Data may be shared only with service providers needed to operate, analyze, secure, or improve the app, or when required by law.

7. Data deletion requests

You may request deletion of data associated with your support request or app usage where technically available. Visit the Data Deletion Request page for instructions.

8. Children’s privacy

CleanSweep is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page with the effective date.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support@cleansweepapp.com
