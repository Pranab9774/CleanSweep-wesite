Data Deletion Request

CleanSweep does not require a user account for core app functionality. If you have contacted support or if app analytics data can be associated with your request, you may ask us to delete eligible data where technically possible.

How to request deletion

Email support@cleansweepapp.com with the subject line “CleanSweep Data Deletion Request”.

Please include

  • Your email address used for support communication.
  • Your device model and Android version, if relevant.
  • A short description of the data you want deleted.

Important note

CleanSweep cannot delete files from your device remotely. Device files remain under your control and can be managed directly on your Android device.
